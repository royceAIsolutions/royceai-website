## Current Price Action

Ethereum (ETH/USD) is trading at $1,679.02 as of June 9, 2026, down 0.66% from the previous close of $1,690.15. The cryptocurrency has been under significant pressure over the past two months, declining from a 60-day high of $2,421.07 to recent lows around $1,568.77. However, a modest recovery over the past three trading sessions has lifted ETH off its local bottom, with the price climbing from $1,568.77 to the current $1,679.02 — a bounce of approximately 7.0%.

The broader crypto market context shows Bitcoin at $62,773 (-0.50% daily), indicating a relatively stable day with low volatility across the sector. Ethereum's underperformance relative to Bitcoin (BTC dominance expanding) signals that ETH faces unique headwinds — including competition from alternative Layer-1 networks and uncertainty around Layer-2 fee dynamics post-Dencun upgrade.

## RSI Analysis

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) on the 14-day timeframe using Wilder's smoothing method reads 57.6, placing ETH firmly in neutral territory. This represents a meaningful improvement from oversold conditions seen last week when prices tested the $1,568.77 level. The RSI has climbed from approximately 32 (oversold) to 57.6 in just five trading sessions, indicating strong buying momentum on the bounce.

On the weekly timeframe, the RSI sits near 44, suggesting the broader trend remains bearish but not yet oversold enough to trigger a sustainable reversal. A weekly RSI reading below 40 would signal deeper bearish momentum, while a move above 50 on the weekly chart would confirm a trend shift.

The daily RSI's current position offers no extreme signal in either direction. This opens the door for continuation of the current bounce or a resumption of the downtrend — the direction will be determined by price action at key resistance levels.

## MACD Analysis

The MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) reading is deeply bearish, with the MACD line at -140.84 and the signal line at -116.39, producing a negative histogram of -24.45. This indicates that short-term momentum is significantly weaker than medium-term momentum.

However, there are early signs of improvement. The MACD histogram has been narrowing over the past three sessions, moving from approximately -45 to -24.45. If this trend continues, we could see a MACD crossover within 5-7 trading days — a bullish signal that often precedes meaningful trend reversals.

Traders should watch for the MACD line crossing above the signal line as a confirmation trigger. Until that occurs, the path of least resistance remains to the downside.

## Moving Averages

ETH's moving average structure paints a bearish picture. The 20-day Simple Moving Average (SMA20) sits at $1,915.26, with the current price 12.4% below this level — a significant gap that signals genuine bearish alignment. The 50-day SMA at $2,135.62 is even further above, with ETH trading 21.4% below the 50-day benchmark.

Price below both the SMA20 and SMA50 constitutes a classic "death cross" alignment on shorter timeframes. For the trend to turn bullish, ETH must first reclaim the SMA20 at $1,915, which represents a 14% move higher from current levels — a substantial rally but not impossible in crypto markets.

Notably, no moving average crossovers are imminent. The SMA20 is declining at roughly $15 per day, while the SMA50 is declining at approximately $10 per day. If the current bounce continues, we could see price test the SMA20 within 2-3 weeks.

## Volume Analysis

Trading volume over the past 24 hours stands at $17.4 billion, essentially in line with the 20-day average of $17.2 billion (1.01x average). This neutral volume profile suggests that neither buyers nor sellers are aggressively committing capital at current levels.

On Balance Volume (OBV) over the past 60 days shows an upward trend despite the price decline, indicating that smart money has been quietly accumulating ETH during the selloff. This bullish divergence between price and OBV is one of the more constructive signals in the current technical picture.

## Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands on the daily timeframe show the current price ($1,679.02) positioned in the lower half of the bands. The upper band is at $2,285.99 and the lower band is at $1,544.53, producing a band width of 38.7% — relatively wide by historical standards.

## Ichimoku Cloud

The Ichimoku Cloud on the daily chart is bearish. The price is trading well below both the cloud (Senkou Span A/B) and the Tenkan-sen (conversion line). The Chikou Span (lagging span) is also positioned below the cloud, confirming the bearish structure.

The Tenkan-sen at $1,802.15 and the Kijun-sen at $1,915.30 serve as additional resistance levels. For a bullish Ichimoku signal, ETH would need to:
1. Break above the Tenkan-sen ($1,802)
2. Have the Tenkan-sen cross above the Kijun-sen (TKX)
3. Trade above the cloud

This is a medium-term process requiring sustained upward momentum over 2-4 weeks.
