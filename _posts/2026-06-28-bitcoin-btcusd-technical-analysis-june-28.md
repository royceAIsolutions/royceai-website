# Bitcoin (BTC/USD) Technical Analysis — June 28, 2026

Bitcoin is trading at $60,219 on June 28, 2026, down 0.3% from the previous close. The Crypto Fear and Greed Index has plunged to 17 — Extreme Fear territory — suggesting capitulation may be approaching. Historically, Extreme Fear readings have marked local bottoms rather than the start of sustained downtrends.

## Elliott Wave Analysis

The Elliott Wave count from the April 17 peak suggests Bitcoin is in a **corrective Wave C** of an A-B-C structure:

- **Wave A:** $77,127 to $68,900 (April 17 to May 2) — 10.7% decline
- **Wave B:** $68,900 to $73,800 (May 2 to May 20) — 7.1% recovery
- **Wave C:** $73,800 to current — targeting $58,000-$60,000

Wave C typically extends to 1.618 times the length of Wave A, which projects a target of approximately $58,500. This aligns precisely with the high-volume support node identified in the volume analysis. If Wave C completes at $58,500, a new impulsive bullish wave structure could begin targeting new highs above $77,000.

An alternative count suggests this could be a larger-degree Wave 4 correction from the January low, which would target the $54,000-$56,000 zone (40-50% retracement of Wave 3).
