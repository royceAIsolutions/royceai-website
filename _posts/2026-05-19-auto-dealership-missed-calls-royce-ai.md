## Why Human Receptionists Can't Keep Up
Traditional dealerships rely on human receptionists to answer calls, but this model has fundamental flaws that AI solves. First, human staff work fixed hours. Even dealerships open until 8 PM have 12+ hours of downtime every day where calls go unanswered. For customers shopping after work or on weekends, this gap means lost leads.

Second, human receptionists can only handle one call at a time. During peak hours (10 AM to 2 PM, according to NADA), dealerships often get 5-10 simultaneous calls. Even the most efficient receptionist can't answer all of them, leading to busy signals or long hold times that drive buyers away.

Third, staffing shortages are worsening. The auto industry faces a 22% turnover rate for front-desk staff, per a 2026 Deloitte report. Training new receptionists takes weeks, and even experienced staff get sick, take vacations, or quit, leaving gaps in coverage that cost dealerships thousands in lost leads.
