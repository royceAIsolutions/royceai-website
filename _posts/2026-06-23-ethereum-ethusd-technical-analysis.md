# Ethereum ETHUSD Technical Analysis June 23 2026: Key Levels to Watch

Ethereum has entered a critical juncture after a punishing selloff that dragged prices from the 2,900 dollar region down to test the 1,600 dollar support zone. As of June 23, 2026, ETHUSD is trading near 1,650 dollars, down approximately 5.4 percent on the day and nursing losses of nearly 30 percent over the past 24 hours on derivatives markets. The broader market structure remains bearish on higher timeframes, but short-term indicators suggest that buyers are beginning to defend a major demand zone. This analysis examines the full technical picture across multiple indicators and timeframes to identify the key levels that will determine Ethereum's next major move.

## Current Price Action

Ethereum's price action over the past month has been characterized by a series of lower highs and lower lows, confirming the bearish trend that took hold after the failed breakout above 2,900 dollars in early June. The initial rejection at that level was sharp, with a cascade of liquidations accelerating the decline. The 2,800 dollar level provided brief support before giving way, followed by the 2,500 dollar psychological level which also failed to hold.

The most significant development is the current test of the 1,600 dollar region, which represents a major demand zone that previously served as a launching point for the rally earlier this year. Price action around this level will be critical. A decisive break below 1,600 dollars would open the door to a move toward 1,400 dollars and potentially lower. Conversely, a strong bounce from this zone could signal the beginning of a relief rally targeting the 1,800 to 1,900 dollar resistance range.

Volume on the decline has been above average, which is typical for capitulation-style selling. However, the most recent sessions have shown declining volume on the selloff, suggesting that selling pressure may be exhausting itself. This divergence between price and volume is worth monitoring closely in the coming days.

## RSI Analysis

The Relative Strength Index on the daily timeframe has dropped to 28, firmly in oversold territory. Historically, RSI readings below 30 on Ethereum have preceded meaningful bounces, particularly when they occur at major support levels. The last time RSI reached these levels was during the March 2026 correction, which was followed by a 45 percent rally over the subsequent three weeks.

On the 4-hour timeframe, RSI is showing early signs of bullish divergence. While price has made a new low, the RSI has formed a higher low, indicating that bearish momentum is weakening. This is a classic setup for a short-term reversal, though it requires confirmation from price action.

The weekly RSI, however, tells a more cautionary tale. At 42, it has room to decline further before reaching oversold conditions on that timeframe. This suggests that while a short-term bounce is likely, the broader trend may not have fully exhausted its downside potential. Traders should be cautious about interpreting the daily oversold signal as a full trend reversal.

## MACD Analysis

The Moving Average Convergence Divergence on the daily chart shows the MACD line well below the signal line, with the histogram printing increasingly negative values. This confirms the bearish momentum that has dominated price action throughout June. However, the rate of decline in the histogram has begun to slow, which often precedes a MACD crossover.

On the 4-hour chart, the MACD is showing early signs of convergence. The MACD line is beginning to flatten relative to the signal line, and the histogram bars are shrinking in size. If this continues, a bullish crossover on the 4-hour MACD could provide the catalyst for a short-term bounce toward the 1,750 to 1,800 dollar range.

The weekly MACD remains in bearish territory with no signs of convergence yet. This reinforces the view that any bounce from current levels should be treated as a counter-trend move within a larger bearish structure, rather than the start of a new uptrend.

## Moving Averages

Ethereum's moving average configuration is unambiguously bearish across all timeframes. On the daily chart, the 20-day exponential moving average sits at approximately 2,100 dollars, well above the current price. The 50-day SMA is at 2,350 dollars, and the 100-day SMA is near 2,600 dollars. The 200-day SMA, often considered the line in the sand for long-term trend determination, is at approximately 2,450 dollars.

The fact that price is trading below all four major moving averages confirms the bearish trend structure. The 20-day EMA will likely act as the first line of resistance on any bounce, with the 200-day SMA representing a more significant overhead barrier. A move back above the 200-day SMA would be required to shift the long-term trend back to bullish.

The moving averages are also in a bearish alignment, with the 20-day EMA below the 50-day SMA, which is below the 100-day SMA, which is below the 200-day SMA. This stacked bearish configuration is a textbook downtrend signal and suggests that sellers remain firmly in control of the market.

## Volume Analysis

Daily trading volume has been elevated during the decline, which is consistent with distribution. The On-Balance Volume indicator has been trending steadily lower since the 2,900 dollar peak, confirming that selling pressure has been dominant throughout the correction.

However, the Volume Price Trend indicator has begun to flatten in recent sessions, even as price has continued to decline. This subtle divergence suggests that the intensity of selling is beginning to wane. In healthy downtrends, volume expands on declines and contracts on bounces. The recent contraction in selling volume could indicate that the market is approaching a selling climax.

The open interest data from derivatives markets adds another layer of context. Ethereum's open interest has declined 26 percent over the past 30 days and 7.7 percent over the past week, indicating that leveraged long positions have been systematically liquidated. This deleveraging process often marks the latter stages of a correction, as the removal of excessive leverage sets the stage for a more sustainable price recovery.

## Support and Resistance Levels

The key support levels for ETHUSD are as follows. Immediate support sits at 1,600 dollars, which represents the major demand zone discussed above. Below that, 1,500 dollars serves as psychological support, followed by the 1,400 dollar level which corresponds to the January 2026 lows. A break below 1,400 dollars would expose the 1,250 dollar region, which represents the next major structural support.

On the resistance side, 1,740 to 1,800 dollars represents the first significant overhead barrier. This zone previously acted as support before the recent breakdown and will likely attract selling pressure. Above that, 1,950 to 2,000 dollars represents a more substantial resistance zone, corresponding to the 200-day moving average and a previous consolidation area. The 2,200 to 2,300 dollar range marks the next major resistance, aligning with the 100-day SMA.

The 2,500 dollar level remains the critical resistance that must be reclaimed to shift the intermediate-term trend back to neutral. Until that level is reached, the path of least resistance remains to the downside.

## Chart Patterns

The daily chart shows a descending channel pattern that has been in place since the 2,900 dollar peak. The upper boundary of this channel connects the June high with subsequent lower highs, while the lower boundary connects the corresponding lows. Price is currently testing the lower boundary of this channel, which typically provides support for a bounce back toward the channel's midpoint.

A measured move analysis based from the channel suggests that a breakdown below the lower boundary would target 1,350 to 1,400 dollars. Conversely, a successful defense of the channel support could trigger a rally back toward the channel's upper boundary near 2,000 to 2,100 dollars.

On the 4-hour chart, a potential double bottom pattern is forming around the 1,600 dollar level. If confirmed by a move above the intermediate high near 1,750 dollars, this pattern would project a target of approximately 1,900 dollars.

## Fibonacci Retracement

Applying Fibonacci retracement to the rally from the December 2025 low of 1,100 dollars to the June 2026 high of 2,900 dollars reveals key levels. The 38.2 percent retracement sits at 2,200 dollars, which aligns with the moving average resistance zone discussed above. The 50 percent retracement is at 2,000 dollars, and the 61.8 percent retracement, often considered the golden ratio level, is at 1,800 dollars.

The current price action is testing the 78.6 percent retracement level near 1,580 dollars. This deep retracement level often acts as the final line of defense before a full retracement of the entire move. A break below 1,580 dollars would suggest that the market is not merely correcting the previous rally but potentially beginning a new downtrend.

## Bollinger Bands

The daily Bollinger Bands have expanded significantly during the recent decline, reflecting the increase in volatility. Price is currently trading near the lower Bollinger Band, which is consistent with the oversold RSI reading. Historically, touches of the lower Bollinger Band during established downtrends often result in a bounce back toward the middle band, currently near 2,100 dollars.

The Bollinger Band width has reached levels not seen since the March 2026 correction, indicating that volatility is elevated. This typically precedes a period of consolidation as the market digests the recent move. Traders should watch for a contraction in the band width as a signal that the market is preparing for its next directional move.

## Ichimoku Cloud

The Ichimoku Cloud analysis confirms the bearish picture. Price is trading well below the cloud, with the cloud itself shifting from green to red on the daily chart. The Tenkan-sen is below the Kijun-sen, and the Chikou Span is below price action, all confirming bearish momentum.

The cloud's lower boundary sits near 2,300 dollars, which represents significant overhead resistance. For the Ichimoku to flash a bullish signal, price would need to move above the cloud, the Tenkan-sen would need to cross above the Kijun-sen, and the Chikou Span would need to move above price. None of these conditions are currently met, reinforcing the bearish outlook.

## Elliott Wave Analysis

From an Elliott Wave perspective, Ethereum appears to be in the midst of a corrective wave following the impulse wave that carried prices from 1,100 dollars to 2,900 dollars. The current decline could represent either a Wave C of an A-B-C correction or the early stages of a new bearish impulse wave.

If this is a Wave C completion, the 1,580 to 1,600 dollar zone represents a high-probability reversal area, and a new impulse wave to the upside could begin from current levels. If, however, this is the beginning of a new bearish impulse, the current bounce would be a Wave 2 correction before a more severe Wave 3 decline targets the 1,200 to 1,300 dollar region.

The key differentiator will be the structure of the bounce. A sharp, impulsive bounce would support the Wave C completion thesis. A sluggish, choppy bounce would suggest a Wave 2 correction within a new downtrend.

## Market Sentiment

The cryptocurrency Fear and Greed Index currently reads 24, firmly in Extreme Fear territory. Historically, readings in this range have coincided with local bottoms, as panic selling tends to exhaust itself at these sentiment extremes. The last time the index reached 24 was during the March 2026 correction, which preceded a significant rally.

Funding rates on Ethereum perpetual futures have turned negative, with the average weighted funding rate at -0.002278. This means shorts are paying longs, which is a contrarian bullish signal. Negative funding rates during extreme fear often mark capitulation events where the last remaining longs have been liquidated.

The put-call ratio for Ethereum options has spiked to 1.8, indicating that put volume significantly exceeds call volume. While this reflects the current bearish sentiment, extreme put skew has historically preceded relief rallies as the market becomes overly positioned to the downside.

## Trading Recommendations

For swing traders, the current setup presents a high-risk, high-reward opportunity. The confluence of oversold RSI, major support at 1,600 dollars, extreme fear sentiment, and negative funding rates creates a favorable risk-reward for a long position. Entry could be considered in the 1,600 to 1,650 dollar range with a stop loss below 1,550 dollars. Initial targets are 1,800 dollars and 2,000 dollars, with a stretch target of 2,200 dollars if the bounce gains momentum.

For position traders and long-term investors, the message is more cautious. The weekly trend remains bearish, and the moving average structure suggests that any bounce should be viewed as an opportunity to reduce exposure rather than add to positions. A weekly close above 2,300 dollars would be required to shift the long-term outlook back to neutral.

Short sellers should exercise caution at current levels. While the trend is bearish, the extreme oversold conditions and capitulation signals suggest that the near-term downside may be limited. Better shorting opportunities may present themselves on a bounce toward the 1,800 to 2,000 dollar resistance zone.

## Risk Factors

Several factors could invalidate the bullish bounce scenario. A breakdown below 1,550 dollars would signal that the selling is not exhausted and could accelerate toward 1,400 dollars and below. Broader market weakness, particularly in Bitcoin, could drag Ethereum lower regardless of its own technical setup. Regulatory developments, particularly around Ethereum ETF approvals or rejections, could introduce unexpected volatility.

On the upside, a surprise move above 1,800 dollars with strong volume would shift the short-term bias to neutral and open the door to a more sustained rally. Positive developments in the Ethereum ecosystem, such as major protocol upgrades or institutional adoption announcements, could provide fundamental catalysts for a trend reversal.

## Conclusion

Ethereum stands at a critical crossroads. The technical picture is bearish on higher timeframes, but short-term indicators are flashing oversold signals at a major support level. The 1,600 dollar zone represents the line in the sand for bulls. A successful defense here could trigger a meaningful relief rally toward 1,800 to 2,000 dollars. A breakdown would open the door to a more severe decline toward 1,400 dollars. Traders should watch volume patterns and the 4-hour MACD for early signals of which scenario is playing out. The extreme fear sentiment and negative funding rates suggest that the market is approaching a turning point, but patience and confirmation are essential before committing to a directional bias.
