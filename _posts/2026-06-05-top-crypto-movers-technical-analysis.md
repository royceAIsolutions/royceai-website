# Top Crypto Movers Technical Analysis — June 5, 2026

The cryptocurrency market experienced a heavily bearish session on Friday, June 5, 2026. Bitcoin appears to be in a Wave 4 correction of a larger-degree impulsive structure that began from the early 2026 lows. If this wave count is correct, Wave 4 is typically a 23.6%-38.2% retracement of Wave 3, suggesting a potential low near $72,500-$75,000. The current corrective structure shows ABC sub-waves, with wave C potentially completing near the 50-day SMA.

Ethereum's wave structure is more complicated. The asset appears to have completed an ABC correction that extended beyond typical retracement levels. If the count is corrective, ETH could be in an ending diagonal or extended flat pattern. A clear 5-wave rally off the current support zone would confirm the larger uptrend is intact.

Solana exhibits a clear ABC corrective structure from the October 2025 high. Wave A drove price from $175 to $110, Wave B retraced to $140, and Wave C has been the extended selloff to current levels. Wave C appears to be completing as it approaches the 1.618 Fibonacci extension of Wave A at approximately $77.
