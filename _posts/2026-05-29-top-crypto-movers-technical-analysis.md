## Risk Factors

Several risk factors could impact the cryptocurrency market in the near term:

1. **Regulatory Uncertainty:** Ongoing discussions in Washington regarding cryptocurrency regulation could create sudden volatility. Any negative news could trigger sharp selloffs.

2. **Macroeconomic Factors:** Federal Reserve interest rate decisions and inflation data releases can significantly impact risk assets including cryptocurrencies.

3. **Bitcoin Dominance Decline:** Bitcoin dominance has dropped to 52%, indicating capital rotation into altcoins. While this benefits SOL, ADA, and MATIC in the short term, a Bitcoin breakdown could drag the entire market lower.

4. **Liquidation Cascades:** High leverage in the derivatives market creates risk of cascading liquidations if key support levels break, particularly for Bitcoin around 93,800 dollars.

5. **Technical Pattern Failures:** The symmetrical triangle on Bitcoin and bull flag on Ethereum could fail, leading to false breakouts and trapped traders.
