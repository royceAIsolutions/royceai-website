## Why Dental Practices Miss Calls

### Peak Hour Overload

Most missed calls happen during peak office hours, particularly **10-11 AM** and **lunch hours**. During these periods, front desk staff are checking in patients, handling insurance verification, managing payments, and juggling multiple responsibilities — phones ring unanswered.

### Limited Staff Bandwidth

Dental practices typically meet only 60% of necessary phone coverage during peak times. A single front desk person cannot simultaneously check in a patient in-chair, handle a walk-in, and answer three ringing lines.

### After-Hours Gaps

After 5 PM and on weekends — when many patients are free to call — nearly 100% of calls go to voicemail. Yet **after-hours calls convert at higher rates** because patients who call outside business hours are actively seeking care.
