## Risk Factors

Several risk factors could invalidate the technical analysis and cause significant losses:

1. **Leverage Decay:** TQQQ and SQQQ suffer from beta slippage (volatility decay) over time. In sideways markets, both ETFs lose value due to the mathematics of daily resetting leverage. Long-term holders must monitor this decay carefully.

2. **Federal Reserve Policy:** Unexpected hawkish statements from the Federal Reserve could trigger a sharp selloff in technology stocks, causing TQQQ to gap down and SQQQ to gap up. The Nasdaq-100 is particularly sensitive to interest rate changes.

3. **Earnings Season:** Major Nasdaq-100 components (Apple, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Amazon, Meta) report earnings quarterly. Negative surprises from any of these heavyweights can cause TQQQ to decline sharply and SQQQ to rally.

4. **Geopolitical Events:** Unexpected geopolitical developments (wars, trade disputes, regulatory actions) can cause gap moves that bypass technical support and resistance levels entirely.

5. **Liquidity Risk:** While TQQQ and SQQQ are highly liquid during regular market hours, pre-market and after-hours trading can experience wider spreads and lower liquidity, increasing slippage on stop-loss orders.
