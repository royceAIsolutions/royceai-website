Today, I'm excited to announce the launch of RoyceAI — an autonomous AI operations agent that handles your business's inbound calls, texts, emails, and lead nurturing entirely on its own. No humans in the loop. No missed follow-ups. No leads falling through the cracks.

The Problem

Every business with a phone number or email address is drowning in inbound communication. Missed calls go to voicemail. Voicemails never get returned. Emails pile up. Leads sit for days. By the time you get back to someone, they've already moved on to a competitor who answered.

The numbers are brutal:

  • 78% of customers choose the first business that responds — speed is everything.
  • 55% of leads are never followed up with at all.
  • The average small business misses 40+ calls per week during off-hours.

Most businesses can't afford a 24/7 receptionist. But every business can afford an AI that works for pennies per interaction.

What RoyceAI Does

RoyceAI answers every inbound call — day or night, weekend or holiday. It speaks naturally, qualifies leads using your criteria (BANT or custom), schedules meetings, and logs every interaction to your CRM. It also handles SMS and email, following up on the channel your lead prefers.

Behind the scenes, RoyceAI runs persistent, multi-step nurture sequences. If a lead doesn't respond to SMS in 3 days, it follows up by email. If there's still no response, it calls. The system never forgets, never gets tired, and never drops a ball.

"Grant Cardone said on camera that he wants an AI receptionist. RoyceAI already is exactly that."

Why Now

We've reached an inflection point in AI voice technology. Models are natural enough that callers can't tell they're talking to an AI — and more importantly, they don't care, because their problem gets solved instantly. The tech stack has matured: voice synthesis is indistinguishable from human speech, language models handle complex conversations reliably, and integration APIs connect to every major CRM and calendar system.

RoyceAI sits at this intersection. It's not a demo or a prototype. It's a live system handling real calls on a real phone number, right now.
